Living in NORWAY

Moving to Norway is a desire that many people share. My husband and me had the chance to do it last year, in November 2021. To be honest, it is one of the greatest periods of my life and I really love my life in Norway, even if the adjustments are sometimes harsh and difficult.

Christmas in NORWAY

The main Christmas celebration is on Christmas Eve; the evening of December 24. People in Norway normally celebrate Christmas Eve with their close family, gathering for the Christmas dinner meal. Walk around the Christmas tree after dinner is an old tradition in Norway.
